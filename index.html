Cinnamon Raisin Soda Bread

  1. 4 cups flour
  2. 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  3. 1 1/2 tsp salt
  4. 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  5. several handfuls raisins
  6. several handfuls coconut (optional)
  7. 1 3/4 cups buttermilk (or until right texture)
  • Preheat oven 420 degrees.
  • Mix together dry ingredients, then add the buttermilk.
  • Bake 30 min with metal bowl covering.
  • Remove bowl and bake an additional 20 min.
